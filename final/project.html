Magical Places Inspired by Harry Potter

Site Name

Magical Places Inspired by Harry Potter

This name clearly represents the focus of the site on magical real-world locations connected to the Harry Potter universe.

Site Purpose

This website provides Harry Potter fans with information about real places around the world that inspired or resemble locations from the magical series. It offers photos, historical context, and travel tips to help visitors explore these enchanting destinations.

Scenarios

  • Which castles or locations resemble Hogwarts the most?
  • How can I plan a trip to visit these magical places and what should I know before going?

Color Schema

  • #7B3F00 - Reddish brown used for headings and accents.
  • #4B2E05 - Dark brown used for main text to ensure readability.
  • #F5EFE6 - Light beige background for a warm, magical feel.

Typography

  • ‘Poppins’ (Google Font) - Used for headings to give a modern and friendly look.
  • ‘Segoe UI’ - Used for body text for readability and wide compatibility.

Wireframe

Mobile View: Simple vertical layout with site title on top, followed by navigation links, main content sections stacked, and footer.

Desktop View: Header with logo and navigation on top, a main content area with a sidebar for quick links or featured places, and a footer at the bottom.
